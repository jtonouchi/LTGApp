# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 2, 2026**

As you use this app to reflect, pray, and grow, your privacy matters. This policy explains how your information is handled with care and simplicity.

## 1. Information You Enter

The app allows you to record:

*	LTG meeting notes
*	Daily Scripture meditation responses
*	Weekly reflection questions
*	Prayer requests

These entries may contain personal or confidential information. You remain in full control of what you choose to write.

## 2. Local-Only Data Storage

All information is stored **solely on your device**.

The app does not:

*	Collect personal data
*	Transmit data to servers
*	Share data with third parties
*	Use analytics or tracking tools

Your reflections stay with you.

## 3. No Accounts or Identity Tracking

The app does not create accounts, request login credentials, or gather identifying information. You may use the app anonymously at all times.

## 4. Data Deletion

You may delete individual entries using the swipe-to-delete gesture.

If you uninstall the app, all stored data will be permanently removed from your device.

## 5. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because no data is collected or transmitted, the app aligns naturally with child\uc0\u8209 safety expectations.

## 6. Security

Because all data remains on your device, its security depends on your device\'92s settings and protections (such as passcodes or biometric locks). Please safeguard your device accordingly.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be reflected in the version published within the App Store listing.

## 8. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about these terms, please contact the developer through the contact information provided in the App Store listing.
